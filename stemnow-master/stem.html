Missions and Goals

STEM NOW is an organization dedicated to creating networking opportunities for young women to pursue learning, development, and careers in STEM fields.Through influence, support, and education, STEM NOW advocates for a balanced diversified workforce to participate in advancing the world around us.

STEM NOW connects established women professionals with students and young graduates to provide guidance, ideas, and direction. Furthermore, this organization will create opportunities with the community by encouraging young girls to explore and participate in science and mathematics and prepare them to be confident and valued in pursuing their aspirations.
